# LLM Wiki (gist)

## Summary in 3 bullets

- Prefer a **persistent, interlinked wiki** over pure RAG for compounding knowledge.
- Three layers: raw sources, wiki, schema (agent instructions).
- Operations: ingest, query (file answers back), lint.

## Why I saved this

Foundation for kheMind’s wiki layer and [Karpathy LLM Wiki pattern](../../meta/karpathy-llm-wiki.md).
